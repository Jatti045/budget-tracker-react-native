Screenshot guidelines and suggestions

Recommended screenshot sizes:

- Phone (portrait): 1080 x 1920 (minimum)
- Tablet (optional): 1200 x 1920

Suggestions:

- Show: dashboard (balances), add transaction flow, budget creation, charts view.
- Use high-contrast captions on screenshots to highlight features.
- Keep background neutral. Export PNGs with sRGB color profile.

Save screenshots into `client/assets/screenshots/` and include at least 3 phone screenshots before submitting.
